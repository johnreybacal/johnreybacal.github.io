Programming tips

So you're a developer like me? I got some tips for you!

Be a good programmer. Good programmers doesn't necessarily mean that you can easily code anything in a short amount of time, for me it is making codes that are clear and easy to understand. Each programming language has it's own coding standards, practice that and your team will love you.

What I love most about programming is that it challenges me. I even prefer finding a bug because it let's me investigate further through the code. Most programmers hates seeing bugs in their code but I think they should see it as an opportunity to improve their skills and their code.
